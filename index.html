Safe Collection

Who are we?

Safe Recollect is a social project created by young students who aim to make a positive change in their city Cuernavaca, Morelos, Mexico

Mission

Contribute to have a cleaner city while improving scavenger's life quality.

Goals

  1. Contribute to SDG 11- Sustainable cities and communities
  2. Contribute to SDG 13- Climate action
  3. Make the garbage collection process faster in our city
  4. Ameliorate scavenger's working conditions
  5. Reduce the accumulation of garbage on the streets of our city

Current project

Our current project consists of collocating different lever trash cans, specifically designed to crush PET and cans. When people throw out their trash in our trashcans they are facilitating the collection process for scavengers.
